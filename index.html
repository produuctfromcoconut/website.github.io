About Us

Natco Coconut Oil are manufacturers of pure coconut oil. They are packed in convenient small consumer packs like pouches, bottles and cans for the purpose of using as edible oil. Manufactured in a modernised unit at Ponoor, Calicut, Kerala.

Coconut Oil is extracted from superior quality malabar copras, processed in our modernized plant using specially developed sudden drying technology. The filtering and fully automatic packing procedure of final product ensure edible grade.
